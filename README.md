## Project Overview

The Super Store Sales Dashboard is an interactive Power BI solution developed to analyze sales performance, profit trends, customer purchasing behavior, and regional business performance. The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of key business metrics, enabling stakeholders to monitor growth, identify profitable segments, and make data-driven decisions.

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

- Total Sales: 1.6M
- Total Quantity Sold: 22K
- Total Profit: 175K
- Average Ship Days: 4
- Regional Sales Performance
- Category & Sub-Category Analysis

## Key Insights

- West region generated the highest share of total sales.
- COD was the most preferred payment method among customers.
- Consumer segment contributed the largest portion of revenue.
- Office Supplies emerged as the top-performing product category.
- Phones generated the highest sales among all sub-categories.
- Sales and profit showed significant growth during the final quarter of the year.
- Standard Class was the most frequently used shipping mode.
